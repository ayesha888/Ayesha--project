Fashion Show

*The Centennial Fashion Show has been Sold Out. We appreciate all the support!* The Centennial Committee is very excited to host one of the first major events to commemorate this year of celebrations. A fashion show titled “Fashion Through The Years” will take place Friday, March 21, 2016 at Elmcrest Banquets in Elmwood Park. This show will prove to be an impressive occasion with hundreds of guests in attendance. Our fashion show committee plans to create beautifully themed baskets which will be raffled off during this event. Unique boutique purveyors will be selling their finest products and stunning accessories for you to purchase prior to the show. Ticket price is $50.00. Plan ahead and start asking family and friends if they would like to attend so you can reserve a table of 10. The boutique and cash bar will open at 4:30 p.m. Dinner will be served at 6:30 p.m. The fashion show will begin at 7:30 p.m. Valet parking is included in the ticket price. All guests have a chance to win a door prize! This fashion show will be a spectacular event. Don’t miss it!!!.
